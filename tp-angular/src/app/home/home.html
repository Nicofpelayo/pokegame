Welcome to Pokémon Safari!

🎯 Objective

Catch as many Pokémon as you can using your limited Pokéballs. But watch out! There's a 1% chance a Pokémon will appear in its shiny form!

🌿 Safari Zone

Head into the Safari Zone to discover wild Pokémon. You'll see 4 random Pokémon each time. Use your Pokéballs wisely — they are limited!

📘 Pokédex

Your Pokédex keeps track of all the Pokémon you've caught. If you catch a shiny Pokémon, it will also appear in your Shiny Pokédex!

📝 Share your thoughts

Use the feedback form to tell us what you think of the game or suggest improvements!

Good luck, Trainer! 🔥
